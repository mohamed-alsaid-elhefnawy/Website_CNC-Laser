Project Vision

CNC Laser 3018 is a compact desktop machine used for engraving and cutting lightweight materials using a laser beam controlled by computer systems. It provides high precision and is widely used in small-scale manufacturing and educational applications.

Machine Specifications

Parameter Specification
Effective Work Area 300 x 180 x 45 mm
Laser Power 2.5W – 5.5W
Power Supply 12V
Accuracy ±0.1 mm

Main Components

Aluminum Frame

A high-strength structural skeleton made from precision-machined aluminum extrusions for maximum rigidity and vibration damping.

Stepper Motors (NEMA 17)

NEMA 17 motors providing high-torque, precise motion control along the X, Y, and Z axes for complex fabrication paths.

Laser Module

A high-density light source used for focused cutting and high-precision surface engraving on various materials.

ESP MSK DLC32 V2.1

A powerful 32-bit controller board optimized for high-speed motion, stable GRBL firmware execution, and WiFi connectivity.

Working Principle

1

Digital Design

Designs are created in software like LaserGRBL or LightBurn and converted into G-code—a language the machine understands.

2

Signal Processing

The 32-bit controller board processes the G-code and sends precise electrical pulses to the stepper motor drivers.

3

Precision Execution

The motors move the laser head along the X and Y axes while the laser intensity is modulated to engrave or cut the material.
